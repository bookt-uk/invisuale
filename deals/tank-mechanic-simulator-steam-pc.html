Tank Mechanic Simulator (Steam PC)

# Tank Mechanic Simulator If you've ever fancied getting your hands dirty rebuilding vehicles, this simulation offers a surprisingly engaging experience. You'll disassemble and restore World War II tanks piece by piece, using various tools to strip them down to their components before bringing them back to life. The game focuses on authentic mechanical detail, so you'll learn how these historic machines actually work. Once you've finished a restoration, you can test your handiwork on a training ground to make sure everything runs properly. It's a relaxing way to spend an evening if you enjoy methodical, hands-on work. You can either sell your restored tanks for profit or keep them in your own museum. At £1.85, it's worth trying if simulation games appeal to you.
