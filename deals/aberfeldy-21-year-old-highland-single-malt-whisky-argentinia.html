Aberfeldy 21 Year Old Highland Single Malt Whisky ( Argentinian Malbec Wine Cask Finish ) 46% ABV 70cl

Aberfeldy has partnered with Argentinian winery Finca Ambrosia to create this distinctive 21 year old Highland single malt. Distilled back in November 2001, this whisky has spent over two decades maturing in sherry and bourbon casks before receiving a finishing touch in Argentinian Malbec wine casks. The result is a well-rounded dram with added complexity and fruit-forward character from the wine finish. Bottled at 46% ABV, it's presented as a 70cl bottle. Master of Malt currently has this at £108.95, which represents good value for a whisky of this age and provenance. It's a solid choice if you're looking for something with genuine character that goes beyond standard expressions.
