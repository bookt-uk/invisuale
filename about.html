About Invisuale

What is Invisuale?

Invisuale is a free UK deals site that automatically finds and lists the best deals from across the web — updated every day at 9am. We cover Electronics, Gaming, Groceries, Fashion, Home & Living and more.

We do the searching so you don't have to.

How It Works

  • Deals are sourced daily from trusted UK deal communities and retailers
  • Each deal is reviewed and described using AI to give you a clear summary
  • Expired deals are automatically removed
  • Clicking "Get this deal" takes you directly to the retailer — no middleman

Our Story

Invisuale started in 2026 out of frustration. As a UK shopper, I was tired of scrolling through dozens of deal sites stuffed with expired offers, fake "was/now" prices, and US-focused content that didn't apply to British retailers. I wanted one clean place that showed only live UK deals — no clickbait, no inflated discounts, no foreign Amazon links.

So I built it. Invisuale runs on a daily automation that pulls the highest-voted deals from the UK deal community, filters out anything that's already expired, and presents each one with a clear price, the actual retailer, and an honest delivery cost. No fluff.

The site is run solo from the UK. If you've spotted a deal we should know about, or something that needs fixing, get in touch.

How We Pick Deals

Every deal you see on Invisuale has been through three filters:

  • Community validation — Deals are sourced from HotUKDeals, the UK's largest deal community (1M+ active users). Only deals that have gathered significant upvotes from real shoppers make it through. This filters out marketing fluff and "deals" that aren't actually deals.
  • Live verification — Before any deal is published, our scraper hits the retailer's page and verifies the offer is still live. Expired deals never make it onto the site. Already-published deals are re-checked daily and removed the moment they stop working.
  • Honest pricing — We show the actual price, the actual delivery cost (free or otherwise), and the actual retailer. No invented "was" prices, no inflated discount percentages.

Editorial Standards

We hold ourselves to the following standards on every deal and article we publish:

  • Accuracy first — Prices and availability are verified at the time of publication. If a deal expires or sells out, it is removed within 24 hours.
  • No fake urgency — We don't manufacture "1 left!" or "ends in 5 minutes" countdowns. The deal community's vote count and the retailer's own stock indicator are the only signals we surface.
  • No bias from commissions — Our affiliate commission rate has zero influence on which deals appear or how they're ranked. We rank by deal community vote, full stop. We earn commission from some retailers and not others — it doesn't change what we show you.
  • Plain English — Product summaries are written to be readable, not to game search engines. If a description is unclear, email us and we'll rewrite it.
  • Corrections policy — Spotted an error? Email hello@invisuale.com. We correct mistakes within 24 hours and note significant corrections at the bottom of the affected page.

Affiliate Disclosure

Invisuale participates in affiliate programmes including Amazon Associates UK, Awin, and other retailer affiliate networks. This means we may earn a small commission when you click through to a retailer and make a purchase. There is no extra cost to you — the price you pay is exactly the same.
